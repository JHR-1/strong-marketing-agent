# Strong Group Social Media Image Style Guide

## Based on 12 approved reference images from ChatGPT 5.5

### Overall Layout (1080x1080 portrait-style, roughly 4:5 ratio)
- **Background**: Dark navy (#0A1628 to #1A2744) as the dominant base colour
- **Top section**: Category/awareness day badge in top-left corner (rounded rectangle pill shape with coloured border — gold for general, green for environment, red for health, purple for loneliness, cyan for transport)
- **Headline**: MASSIVE bold uppercase text, typically 2-3 lines. Main words in white, one key word in a contrasting accent colour (gold/orange, green, red, cyan, or purple depending on the topic)
- **Body text**: Clean white sans-serif text, left-aligned, 2-3 short paragraphs. Key phrases in accent colour or bold
- **Imagery**: High-quality photorealistic images integrated into the design — construction workers, tools, vehicles, sites. NOT stock photo feel — more editorial/dramatic
- **Decorative elements**: Subtle gold/coloured curved wave separator line above the contact strip. Some posts use small icons (line-art style) to reinforce themes
- **Contact strip at bottom**: Dark navy bar with the Strong Group logo (blue dots pattern + "STRONG GROUP" text) on the left, then three columns: PHONE (0208 763 6122), WEBSITE (strong-group.co.uk), EMAIL (info@strong-group.co.uk). Labels in gold/accent colour, values in white

### Specific Post Types

#### Awareness Day Posts (Bike Week, Men's Health Week, Loneliness Week, Windrush Day, Armed Forces Day, World Environment Day, Volunteers' Week)
- Badge at top with the awareness day name
- Relevant icon or emoji-style graphic
- Tie the awareness day back to the recruitment/construction industry
- Emotional, human-centred messaging
- Relevant photorealistic imagery (workers talking, construction sites, themed visuals)

#### Sector Promo Posts (Driving & Transport, Data Centres, Rail & Infrastructure, Fit-Out & Interiors)
- Sector name in badge at top
- Bold action-oriented headline
- Industry-specific imagery (trucks with Strong Group branding, server rooms, rail tracks, office fit-outs)
- Call to action: "APPLY TODAY", "JOIN US TODAY", "CONTACT US TODAY"
- CTA in a bordered button/pill shape

#### Client Review Posts
- "CLIENT REVIEW" as main headline
- 5 gold stars
- Large quotation marks framing the review text
- Attribution line (role + company type)
- Tagline: "We take pride in the quality of our placements"
- Subtle office/industrial background

#### Workforce Planning Posts
- Multi-image grid layout (2x2) showing different sectors
- Each quadrant labelled with sector name + icon
- Central question/CTA headline
- "CONTACT US TODAY" button

### Colour Palette
- **Primary background**: Dark navy #0A1628 to #1A2744
- **Gold/Orange accent**: #F5A623 / #E8952E (headlines, labels, decorative lines)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (body text, secondary headlines)
- **Red accent**: #E53E3E (health topics, emotional emphasis)
- **Green accent**: #38A169 (environment topics)
- **Purple accent**: #805AD5 (loneliness, planning topics)
- **Cyan/Teal accent**: #38B2AC (transport, driving topics)
- **Blue accent**: #4299E1 (general Strong Group blue)

### Typography Style
- Headlines: Extra bold, condensed, uppercase — similar to Impact or Oswald Black
- Body: Clean sans-serif — similar to Inter or Helvetica Neue
- CTA buttons: Bold uppercase in bordered pill shapes
- Contact strip labels: Uppercase, spaced tracking

### Logo
- Strong Group logo: Blue dots pattern (3x2 grid) + "STRONG" (with coloured N and G) + "GROUP" below
- Always bottom-left of contact strip
- Never distorted or recoloured

### Key Rules
1. ALWAYS include the contact strip at the bottom
2. ALWAYS include the Strong Group logo
3. Headlines should be punchy, 2-5 words max
4. Body text should be recruitment/industry focused
5. Every post should feel premium and professional
6. Imagery should be dramatic and editorial, not generic stock
7. Accent colours should match the topic/theme
8. The wave/curve separator line above the contact strip should match the post's accent colour
